# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
Foster an open and welcoming environment, free from discrimination and harassment.

## Expected Standards
- Mutual respect in all interactions.
- Constructive and collaborative feedback.
- Inclusive language usage.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or discrimination.
- Offensive or derogatory comments.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement
- Violations will be reviewed by the governance committee.
- Actions may include warning, suspension, or ban.
